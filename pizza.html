Pizza

Description

A delicious, warm, brick oven cheese pizza.

This brick oven cheese pizza will leave you craving more cheesy goodness. The melt in your mouth cheese pairs perfectly with the organic tomato sauce. While that may seem conventional, this one of a kind brick oven style is difficult to reproduce without our instruction and destined to make you addicted.

Having second thoughts? Don't worry, this dish is low calorie and super healthy! All you have to do is follow our instructions step by step and pretend you're not braindead!

Ingredients

  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Garlic Tomato Sauce
  • Dough
  • Oregano
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Love

Steps

  1. Round and flatten the dough
  2. Mix 1 part tomato sauce with one part tomato paste
  3. Add tomato concoction to the flattened dough
  4. Spread the paste evenly on the dough and add the cheese evenly
  5. Insert into oven for 10 minutes
  6. Coat crust with olive oil, insert back into oven for 5 minutes
  7. Finito.
